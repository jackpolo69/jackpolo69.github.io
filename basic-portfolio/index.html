About Me

Born and raised in Corona/Jackson Heights, Queens NY, this talanted music producer has a been a part of the Hip Hop culture since 1978. He first started as a DJ at the tender age of nine, and went on to develope his skills by battling other local DJ's, gaining him recognition throughout the borough of queens. At sixteen, he gained interest in persuing an education in the art of Audio-Engineering, enrolling himself into what at the time was the creme de la creme of schools for Audio Engineering; Center For The Media Arts, located in New York City. Here, he became good friends with his instructor James who is a music producer and taught Machavelli all about music production.

Upon graduating, he landed a job as an entry level intern at the most prestigus Hip-Hop studio of its time; "Power Play Studios". Anyone who was relivent in hip-Hop recorded there. This studio became his second home, and he went on to form a Hip-Hop Band; "Smokin Suckaz Wit Logic". After a short period of time and just six NYC shows under their belts, The band created a bidding war in the music industry, which resulted in a major recording and publishing contract with Sony/Epic Music, Entertainment and Sony/BMG Publishing. In 1993, They released their debut album; "Play'n Foolz".

Several years later, Machavelli moved on due to creative differences,forming a two man music production team; "Prince & Machavelli". Since then they have produced countless tracks in the genre of Hip-Hop and R&B. Machavelli, speerheading all business ventures, landed many industry placements for the duo. They have placed records with the likes of Lloyd Banks, Joell Ortiz, Big Noydd, Keshia Cole, Fantasia to name a few, and have worked with countless others like Missy Elliot, Carl Thomas and many more. Today Machavelli is in the midst of a new embarkment, forming a new production team with family menbers. This team consist of a father and son duo along with a cousin/uncle. The treo are called: "The Compound". Expect to hear many songs in the near future produced by this family teamed indivisuals.www.thecompound.com
